UMRAH JOURNEY / 3rd FEBRUARY 2019

Umrah was an amazing experience. I would love to go there again and again
I'd love to go there with my parents and my whole family.


We went to Makkah first, my family and I then went to Madinah, Masjid An-Nabawi and Masjid Al-Haram.
I miss these Masjids already. The first time ever I felt so close to Nabi Muhammad SAW and Allah SWT.
The first time ever my heart beats in zikir. The first time ever my brain resets itself from internet,
social and all the worldly desires, only thinking of my relationship with Him.



I have so much to learn. The knowledge I gained from university and school days dissipates into nothingness when I looked at Kaabah. To me, my knowledge in Islam is only as big as my little finger. How can I not be embarassed? So, when I was there, in these beautiful Masjids, when I saw Kaabah, I kept crying and crying. Allah SWT has blessed us with so many great amzing awesome things, and it's high time to contribute back.
